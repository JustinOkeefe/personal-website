Mini Read Me

This is a simple To-Do app. You can add entries by clicking the pen FAB and filling out the form modal. To delete entries, click on the applicable trash icon.

At a high level: the app contains a model, a data-service, and a Material table. After creating the model (interface), the data is made available via an injectable service. The to-do list component consumes the data, displaying it via the Material table
